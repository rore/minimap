## Summary

Push board editing past the minimal v1 authoring flow with richer manipulation and higher-throughput interactions.

## Why

Once create and basic board editing exist, the next pressure will be speed and convenience rather than capability gaps.

## In Scope

- drag-and-drop reordering
- keyboard-friendly board management
- faster move and regroup actions

## Out of Scope

- changing canonical ownership away from files
- introducing hidden board state
- collaboration features

## Done When

- board-heavy maintenance work feels efficient for larger roadmaps

## Notes

This should only happen after the basic board editor is solid.
